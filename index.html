Predicting NBA Wins post All-Star Break

The post All-Star stretch is arguably one of the most important parts of an NBA Season. This is the time that each
team has their final roster after the NBA trade deadline. Each team is trying to fight for playoff positioning, or
“fighting” for the worst record and draft lottery positioning. The goal for this project is to predict how many wins
each NBA team will have during this stretch and ultimately, where each team will land in the standings.
